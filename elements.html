EDUCATION BACKGROUND 

 

PRIMARY EDUCATION 

Primary education level, I studied at Sekolah Kebangsaan Bukit Kuang from 2008 to 2013. I also graduated in 2013 by taking the UPSR with grades 2A, 2B, and 1C.

SECONDARY EDUCATION 

The secondary education level, I studied at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Bukit Kuang from 2014 until 2018. In 2016, I took the PT3 test with a grade of 3A, while in 2018, I also took the Sijil Pengajian Malaysia (SPM) test with a grade of 4A, 3B, 1C, and 1D.

TERTIARY EDUCATION 

The tertiary education level, I continued my 6th form studies at SMK Sultan Ismail from 2019 until 2020 by taking the Sijil Tinggi Pengajian Malaysia (STPM) test and obtaining a CGPA of 3.50.After completing my studies in Form 6 at SMK Sultan Ismail, I will continue my studies at Universiti College TATI at the degree level in 2023.
